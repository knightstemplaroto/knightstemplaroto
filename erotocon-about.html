Eastern Regional O.T.O. Conference

About EROTOCON

The Eastern Regional O.T.O. Conference (EROTOCON) is envisioned as a chance to explore the topic of divine sexuality in its expressions across many cultures and traditions, with a specific interest in its intersection with Western occultism. Expressions of this connection through lectures, rituals, discussions, art, music, film and other media will be considered. This event is sponsored by the Tahuti Chapter O.T.O. Committee of Four in the Valley of New York, and will be held on Saturday June 20, 2020.

Seeking Proposals

The Eastern Regional OTO Conference (EROTOCON) is seeking proposals for its inaugural event to be held on Saturday June 20, 2020. The theme of this conference is broadly defined as an exploration of traditions that explore the concepts of divine sexuality, including but not limited to its practices and beliefs, practitioners, histories, and methodologies. Interested parties should provide their prospectus to the conference proposal committee by April 1, 2020, via email to erotocon@knightstemplar-oto.org
