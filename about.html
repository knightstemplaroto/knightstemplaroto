About Knights Templar Oasis

Knights Templar Oasis, is an O.T.O. body serving the Thelemic community from Salem, Massachusetts and the oldest local body in New England.

We teach and practice Thelemic ceremonial magick, celebrate the Gnostic Mass, and are chartered to initiate through the III°.

The interests of our membership range widely across esoteric practices and spiritual systems, but all find unity in their acceptance of the principles of The Book of the Law (Liber AL vel Legis).

Local O.T.O.
